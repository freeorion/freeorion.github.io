WeaponFireEvent encodes a single attack in bout, round by attacker_id owned by attacker_owner_id on target_id with weapon_name of power against shield doing damage.

If shield is negative that implies the weapon is shield piercing.

The use of tuple in the constructor is to keep the number of parameters below 10 which is the maximum that some compilers that emulate variadic templates support.

Return principal faction.

PrincipalFaction is used by UnorderedEvents to sort the display of events by Facton. The principal faction should be the faction most active in the event (i.e. the attacker in a WeaponEvent). It is from the perspective of the viewing_empire_id. Some events like BoutBegin are not associated with any faction.
