Detailed Description

Matches objects that are in systems that could have starlanes added between them and all (not just one) of the systems containing (or that are) one of the objects matched by condition. "Could have starlanes added" means that a lane would be geometrically acceptable, meaning it wouldn't cross any other lanes, pass too close to another system, or be too close in angle to an existing lane.
