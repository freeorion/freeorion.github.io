Detailed Description

Destroys the target object.

When executed on objects that contain other objects (such as Fleets and Planets), all contained objects are destroyed as well. Destroy effects delay the desctruction of their targets until after other all effects have executed, to ensure the source or target of other effects are present when they execute.
