Preconditions:

  • m_planet must be the ID of a populated planet not owned by the issuing empire
  • m_ship must be the the ID of a ship owned by the issuing empire
  • m_ship must be the ID of a ship that can invade and that is in the same system as the planet.

Postconditions:

  • The ship with ID m_ship will be marked to invade the planet with id m_planet during the next turn processing.
