Detailed Description

A combination of names of ShipDesign that can be put together to make a fleet of ships, and a name for such a fleet, loaded from default/scripting/starting_unlocks/fleets.inf ShipDesign names refer to designs listed in default/scripting/ship_designs.

Useful for saving or specifying prearranged combinations of prearranged ShipDesigns to automatically put together, such as during universe creation.
