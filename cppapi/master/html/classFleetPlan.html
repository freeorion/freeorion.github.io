Detailed Description

Prepopulated Fleet, consisting of a name and list of ShipDesign names.

FleetPlans are used for providing Fleets during universe generation or during game events.

Fleet plans are loaded from the default/scripting/starting_unlocks/fleets.inf file, the ShipDesign referenced are liste in default/scripting/ship_designs.
