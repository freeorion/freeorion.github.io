Detailed Description

template<class T>
struct OrValidator< T >

a Validator that performs a logical OR of two validators.

Stores and owns clones of the provided validators in boost::scoped_ptrs. Always calls m_validator_a->Validate(). Only calls m_validator_b->Validate() if the first one throws.
