FO_COMMON_API void Serialize ( Archive &  oa,
const Universe &  universe 
)

Serialize universe to output archive oa.

Warning
Do not try to serialize types that contain longs, since longs are different sizes on 32- and 64-bit architectures. Replace your longs with long longs for portability. It would seem that short of writing some Boost.Serialization archive that handles longs portably, we cannot transmit longs across machines with different bit-size architectures.
